2025 Final Presentations


Here's how to complete your final assessment for Q4, 2025:

Note that for G12 students, this assessment will also count as your final exam grade, and so will require a higher degree of sophisication. Please see your instructor for details.


  1. Access the list of suggested topics here.

  2. Access the instruction sheet and declaration form here.

    Enter your proposed topic and complete the declaration, confirming that you understand the assessment requirements.

    Submit the completed form to your instructor.

  3. Schedule a time for your presentation.

    If you are unable to attend school in person, you may be allowed to submit a video presentation.

  4. Check the assessment rubric and the helpful checklist to guide your preparation.

  5. Provide a thoughtful and interesting 8-10 minutes! (Deliver your presentation!)


Do your best to speak in an engaging manner. Choose a topic that you want to share your insights about.
