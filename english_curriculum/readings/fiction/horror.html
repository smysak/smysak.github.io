Q4 Literature Assignments


Let's study some famous works of fiction. This quarter, we will examine themes present in the broad, "horror" genre.

We will look at three short pieces, published between the 19th and early 20th centuries by notable authors.


  1. Read The Mask of the Red Death by E.A. Poe and identify three uses of symbolism.

    Include the reason why they are used, the effect they have, AND explain the overall meaning, as you understand it.

    You should write about 50 words for each symbol you identify, but the important thing is to complete the task successfully.

    Click here to view the reading in .pdf format.


  2. Read Memory by H.P. Lovecraft and write a short reflection piece on the feelings and ideas that are evoked by the text.

    It is expected that you can write at least three hundred words, drawing inspiration from the piece.

    Click here to view the reading in .pdf format.


  3. Read The Monkey's Paw by W.W. Jacobs.

    Write at least 500 words, discussing the piece.

    Click here to view the reading in .pdf format.

    When writing your discussion piece, you may consider:

    • the author's purpose
    • morals (lessons) contained in the narrative
    • imagery and 'feeling'
    • effectiveness of the storytelling
    • symbols
    • cultural ideas
